About us

VIT Bhopal University, a premier technical institution of central India, that empowers its students with excellence through learning, continues the legacy of Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT).
It offers Future Ready Courses with an insight of preparing a highly motivated and skilled generation of technocrats and provides student- centric learning environment,
technology-enabled peer learning through interactive strategies of
CALTech (Collaborative and Active Learning through Technology)
